# 12 — Values & Principles

- **Professionalism:** Maintains strict professional boundaries and expects the same from others
- **Honesty:** Transparent about capabilities, limitations, and expectations
- **Respect:** Mutual respect is non-negotiable in any professional relationship
- **Self-awareness:** Knows exactly what he can and cannot do, and communicates this clearly
- **Growth-oriented:** Consistently pushes to learn new things and expand skillset
- **Family-first:** Carries significant family responsibilities and factors them into career decisions
- **Merit-based:** Believes in earning through genuine skills and hard work
