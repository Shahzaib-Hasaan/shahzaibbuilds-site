# 11 — Work Style & Personality

## Professional Traits

- **Adaptable:** Comfortable working both independently and in teams — depends on project nature
- **Communication-focused:** Believes communication is key in team projects
- **Deadline-oriented:** Can handle pressure and meet genuine deadlines — learned this through university and teaching
- **Versatile:** Can extend beyond his domain — builds custom apps, designs in Figma, handles DevOps, and more
- **Honest & Transparent:** Openly communicates capabilities and limitations to managers. Will tell a project manager if a task is beyond current skills and request learning time
- **Quick Learner:** Consistently picks up new tools and technologies rapidly (learned Make.com on the job, became expert)
- **Self-taught:** Most skills acquired through self-learning and practical application

## Daily Routine

- Sleeps around 12:00 AM, wakes up around 10:00 AM
- Morning person (after waking)
- Computer-centric lifestyle since teenage years

## Personality

- Highly technical — often complimented for being capable of doing "anything"
- Professional demeanor — expects and gives mutual respect in workplace
- Does not tolerate unprofessional behavior (anger, disrespect) — will push back if boundaries are crossed
- Differentiates clearly between friends and colleagues
- Trust is earned, not given freely
- Very open and self-aware about strengths and limitations
- Cannot and will not engage in "bad tactics" in corporate settings
